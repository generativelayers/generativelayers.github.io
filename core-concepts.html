Design Rules

Must do

  • Keep generated material outside agent state until adopted.
  • Expose denials, warnings, and failures.
  • Record enough context for audit and evaluation.
  • Keep adapters thin.

Must not do

  • Silently insert beliefs.
  • Silently execute generated tool calls.
  • Hide policy failures.
  • Replace the host agent language semantics.
